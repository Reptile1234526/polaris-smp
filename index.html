Server Features

How to Join

Polaris SMP is an application-only server. Here's how to get in.

01
Join the Discord

First, join our Discord server and add FlufffiesMC as a friend.

02
Send a Request

DM FlufffiesMC letting them know you'd like to join Polaris SMP.

03
Answer Some Questions

You'll be asked a few things — do you have a mic? Will you log on weekly? That sort of thing.

04
Get Approved & Play!

If accepted, you'll receive the server details and be welcomed into the community. 🎉

Keep it fun

Server Rules

A few simple rules to keep Polaris SMP a great place for everyone.

⚔️   SMP Rules
I

No Griefing

Do not destroy, damage, or modify other players' builds or belongings without permission.

II

No Bug Abusing or Duping

Exploiting bugs, glitches, or duplication methods is not allowed unless explicitly permitted by staff.

III

No Overswearing or Spamming

Keep chat chill. Excessive profanity and spam make the server less enjoyable for everyone.

IV

Teams: 4 Players Max

No team may have more than 4 members. Keep it fair and competitive for all players.

V

Events Are Announced

Special events like End runs, CTF, and others will be announced when they're happening. Keep an eye on Discord!

VI

Have Fun :DDDD

Most importantly — enjoy yourself, be yourself, and make some memories. That's what Polaris is all about.

⌨️   Discord Rules
I

No Spam or Excessive GIFs

Don't flood channels with messages, images, or GIFs. Keep conversations readable.

II

No Overswearing

Occasional language is fine — just don't make every other word a curse.

III

No Leaking Personal Info

Never share anyone's personal information without their explicit permission. This applies in-game too.

IV

Be Thoughtful & Respectful

Be kind and considerate to fellow members — for the most part, at least 😄
